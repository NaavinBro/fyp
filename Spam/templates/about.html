About AITDS

Artificial Intelligence Threat Detection System (AITDS) is designed to protect users from phishing attacks using advanced AI techniques. Our mission is to provide a simple, effective, and automated way to detect phishing threats.

Our Story

AITDS was developed as a response to the increasing number of phishing attacks. By using the CRISP-DM methodology and TensorFlow, we built a system that efficiently detects phishing attempts, helping individuals and organizations stay safe.

Why AITDS?

We created AITDS to reduce the burden on security teams by automating threat detection. This helps to identify phishing attacks in real time and respond faster, improving overall security efficiency.
